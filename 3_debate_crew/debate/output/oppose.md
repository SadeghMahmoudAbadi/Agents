Legal tender is fundamentally required to serve as a reliable unit of account and store of value. The inherent, acute volatility of decentralized cryptocurrencies renders them disastrous for these essential functions; they cannot be reliably used for setting wages, long-term contracts, or stable pricing, instantly introducing paralyzing risk into everyday commerce and crippling savings.
